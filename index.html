Clown Town

Clown Town is a comedic tale about a clown, Nigel, following the cancellation of his children’s TV show. Nigel provides comic relief as he struggles in the world to achieve his dreams. Nigel’s search for external validation disconnects him from reality. We watch as he experiences his private torment and growing disillusionment, wondering if Nigel will ever attain what he is searching for.
